Robust Monocular Depth Estimation under Challenging Conditions

Stefano Gasperini*,1,2, Nils Morbitzer*,1, HyunJun Jung1, Nassir Navab1, Federico Tombari1,3
* equal contribution, 1 Technical University of Munich, 2 VisualAIs, 3 Google
ICCV 2023
ICCV Paper arXiv Code Translated Images

Detrimental factors to monocular depth estimation in difficult settings from nuScenes. Self-supervised works have issues with textureless areas, reflections, and noise. Supervised ones learn artifacts from the ground truth sensor (LiDAR is shown).

Comparison on nuScenes between the fully-supervised AdaBins without and with our approach, and the self-supervised Monodepth2 without and with our method.
